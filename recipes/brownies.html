Chocolate Brownies

Description

These delicious homemade brownies take only 50 minutes to cook. With a whopping 24 servings, this recipe will keep the whole family happy.

Ingredients

All you need is 1 cup of melted butter, 3 cups of white sugar, 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract, 4 eggs, 1 and a half cups of flour, 1 cup of cocoa powder, 1 teaspoon of salt, 1 cup of chocolate chips.

Steps

The first step in baking these sweet treats is to combine the melted butter, sugar, and vanilla in a bowl. Beat the eggs, one at a time, mixing well until it is thouroughly blended. Step 2 is to sift the flour, cocoa powder, and salt into a bowl. Gradually stir the flour mixture into the egg mixture until blended. Stir the chocolate morels. Spread the batter evenly into the prapared baking dish. The third and final step is to bake for 35-40 minutes at 175 dagrees celcius. Make sure to cool on a rack before cutting.
