Jam Doughnuts

Description

These soft jam doughnuts are a staple for a family with a sweet tooth.

Ingredients

The Ingredients include: 500g flour, 60g caster sugar, 15g fresh yeast, 4 eggs, 2 tsp sea salt, 125g softened unsalted butter, 2 litres sunflower oil, 300g of jam.

Steps

First you must make the dough by mixing all dough products along with water. Next you must add the butter to the dough. Then you must let the dough set overnight in the fridge. Next you must cut the dough into 50g peices. After this you can fry the doughnuts. once they have fried, you put caster sugar on them and put the jam inside them. Once you have completed this, you are finished praparing your doughnuts, and they re ready to eat!
